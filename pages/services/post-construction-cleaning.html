Professional Post-Construction Cleanup in South Florida

Construction and renovation projects leave behind fine dust, drywall residue, paint splatters, and debris that settles on ceilings, vents, fixtures, and every surface in your facility. Standard cleaning crews aren't equipped to handle this level of contamination — you need specialists.

Our post-construction cleaning service removes all traces of construction activity from ceilings, ducts, vents, walls, and surfaces. We prepare your new or renovated space for occupancy with thorough, detail-oriented cleaning that meets the highest standards.

What We Clean

🏗️

Ceiling Dust Removal

Remove construction dust from acoustic tiles, exposed ceilings, beams, and overhead structures.

💨

Duct & Vent Cleaning

Clear construction debris from HVAC systems before they circulate dust throughout your space.

💡

Fixture Cleaning

Clean light fixtures, diffusers, and hardware covered in construction film and dust.

✨

Surface Detailing

Remove paint splatters, adhesive residue, and construction marks from finished surfaces.

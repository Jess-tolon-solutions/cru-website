How It Started

Ceilings R Us began in 1999 with a simple observation: commercial ceilings were being neglected. Restaurant owners focused on their kitchens and dining rooms. Hotel managers prioritized lobbies and guest rooms. But overhead — the ceilings, vents, and ductwork that customers see and breathe through every day — was often forgotten until an inspector pointed it out.

We saw an opportunity to help. By specializing exclusively in ceiling cleaning and restoration, we could develop expertise that general cleaning companies couldn't match. We learned exactly what health inspectors look for. We invested in the right equipment and techniques. And we built a reputation for doing the job right.

Built on Relationships

From the beginning, we've believed that doing good work creates lasting relationships. Many of our earliest clients still work with us today. Their facilities have grown and changed, but they trust us to maintain their ceilings because we've consistently delivered quality results.

That trust is our most valuable asset. It's why we don't cut corners, don't oversell services, and don't make promises we can't keep. When you work with Ceilings R Us, you're working with people who care about getting it right.

Looking Forward

After 25+ years, we're still a family business at heart. We've grown, but we haven't lost sight of what made us successful: honest work, fair prices, and results you can count on. We're proud to be South Florida's trusted ceiling cleaning experts, and we look forward to many more years of helping businesses maintain clean, safe, inspection-ready facilities.

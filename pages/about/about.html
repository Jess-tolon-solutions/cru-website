About Ceilings R Us

25+ years of expert commercial ceiling cleaning and restoration across South Florida. Restaurant and facility compliance is our specialty.

South Florida's Trusted Ceiling Cleaning Experts

Since 1999, Ceilings R Us has helped commercial facilities throughout South Florida maintain clean, inspection-ready ceilings. What started as a small family business has grown into the region's most trusted name in commercial ceiling cleaning, restoration, and maintenance.

We specialize in restaurants, hotels, grocery stores, healthcare facilities, and commercial buildings across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. Our team understands what health inspectors look for, and we deliver the results you need to pass inspections with confidence.

Based in Fort Lauderdale, we're your local experts — responsive, reliable, and committed to doing the job right the first time.
